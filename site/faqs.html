Product & Usage

  • How does HeyJulia.ai work on-site?

    Team members record short voice updates or use guided prompts. The system transcribes, extracts key data like quantities, locations, and work categories, and generates a structured report in seconds.

  • What languages does Julia speak?

    Julia speaks more than 20 languages including English, Spanish, and French. She can seamlessly switch languages during a call to communicate with your multilingual crew members.

  • Do I need to train the model for my company's projects?

    No training required. HeyJulia.ai comes pre-tuned for construction workflows but can adapt to your templates, terminology, and report formats.

  • Can I attach photos or drawings?

    Yes. You can add photos, plan markups, or site sketches alongside your voice report — they're automatically linked and summarized in the final log.

  • How long does it take to generate a report?

    Typically less than a minute. Reports are generated in the background, so most of the time you won't even notice it's happening. The final report for each day is done live on the call, so that you can look at it and verify the results before they are shared with the team.

AI & Accuracy

  • How does the system detect errors or missing data?

    You'll give HeyJulia your plan for each project (typically an Excel or CSV file or just a list of tasks). It uses your plan to check the accuracy of what's being reported and will work to resolve ambiguities during the call.

  • Can I review or edit the report before sharing?

    Yes. The reporter will see an editable copy of the report before submitting. You'll review, edit and approve the report before it is shared with the team.

  • Will the data I provide be shared with others?

    No. Your data is private to your organization. HeyJulia.ai is trained on generic public construction data and your data is not used to train the model.

Customization & Teams

  • Can I customize report sections or categories?

    Absolutely. You can create templates for daily logs, safety inspections, or material tracking — all editable from your dashboard.

  • Can I capture custom things like punch lists or safety meetings?

    Yes. Reports are fully customizable — you can set up templates for punch lists, safety meetings, toolbox talks, inspections, or any other workflow your team needs to capture in the field.

  • Can multiple people contribute to the same report?

    Absolutely. Crews can submit updates under the same project or shift, and HeyJulia.ai automatically merges entries into one unified report.

  • How does team management work?

    Admins can add users or project contacts. A user can create and manage any project within the system. A project contact will only have access to the projects they are associated with.

Insights & Analytics

  • Does HeyJulia.ai provide analytics or dashboards?

    Yes. You can view project trends — labor hours, productivity, task completion and project schedule — all derived from field reports.

  • Can it detect recurring issues or delays automatically?

    Right now HeyJulia.ai is focused on capturing field progress accurately. As it collects more data it will be able to provide more proactive insights and analytics.
