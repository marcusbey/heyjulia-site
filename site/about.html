About us

Built by a father-and-son team with 30+ years of making construction tech contractors actually use.

I'm Phil Ogilby. In 1999, my son Justin and I launched iSqFt.com — now the largest commercial bid network in North America (part of ConstructConnect).

In 2012, we founded STACK Construction Technologies, trusted by thousands of subcontractors across the US and Canada.

Three decades on real jobsites taught us one thing: your superintendent knows exactly what's happening on site — you don't.

  • 1–3 hrs lost per day to paperwork by each superintendent
  • Bare minimum is what typed daily reports usually say
  • Only 30% of projects finish on time and on budget

We built HeyJulia.ai to fix that.

Julia is an AI voice agent that makes two quick calls a day — no apps, no forms, no typing. She talks naturally with your on-site reporter in any language and turns the conversation into live dashboards, daily executive summaries, and early warnings — so you can turn losing projects into winners while there's still time.

We didn't chase an AI trend. We built Julia because we've lived these problems alongside contractors for 30 years.
