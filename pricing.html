FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Julia and our pricing.

  • How does the free trial work?

    Start with 100 minutes free. No credit card required. Julia will make calls to your crews and generate reports so you can see the value immediately. Once you hit 100 minutes, you'll be prompted to upgrade to a paid plan.

  • What counts as a minute?

    Each call Julia makes to your crews counts toward your monthly minutes. Both morning check-ins and afternoon progress calls are included in your usage.

  • Can I change plans anytime?

    Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle, and we'll prorate any differences.

  • What if I go over my minutes?

    Overage minutes are billed at the per-minute rate shown on your plan ($0.56, $0.52, or $0.50 depending on tier). You'll receive notifications as you approach your limit, and can upgrade to get a better overage rate.
